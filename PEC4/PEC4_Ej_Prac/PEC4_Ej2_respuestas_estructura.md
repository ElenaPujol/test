      * tsconfing.app.json
      * angular.json
      * package.json
      * .editorconfig,
      * .gitignore
      * …
    * Directorio node_modules
    * Directorio src:
      * index.html
      * main.ts
      * styles.css
      * test.ts
      * polyfills.ts
      * Directorio environments
      * Directorio assets
      * Directorio app
